Full Game Rules: The Indisputable

The game begins with each player placing their token on the starting square, launching the application, and selecting a first player. To leave the central square and enter the path, the player freely chooses a theme, selects a difficulty level (Easy, Intermediate, Hard), answers a question, and, if correct, moves forward and chooses which branch of the circle to follow.

Once on the circular path, the theme of each question is automatically determined by the color of the square the player is standing on. During each turn, the player selects a difficulty level, answers a question displayed by the application (with 30 seconds to choose from three options), and then moves forward depending on the chosen difficulty: 1 square for an easy question, 2 for medium, and 3 for hard. If the answer is wrong, the player does not move.

Some spaces are Chance Squares: if a player lands on one, they must follow the instruction indicated, which may offer an advantage (move forward, bonus) or a disadvantage (move back, lose a turn). The first player to complete a full lap of the circle and return to the center wins the game.

Key elements:

  • Objective: The winner is the first player to complete a full lap and return to the Starting Square.
  • Required Equipment: Board, tokens, mobile phone, and the mandatory Digital Add-on (application).
